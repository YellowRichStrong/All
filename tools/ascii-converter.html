How to Use ASCII Converter

  • To convert text to ASCII: Enter text in the left box, select format and separator, then click "Text to ASCII"
  • To convert ASCII to text: Enter ASCII codes in the right box, select format, then click "ASCII to Text"
  • ASCII codes are separated by spaces, commas, or newlines
  • Supported formats: Decimal (0-127), Hexadecimal (00-7F), and Binary (00000000-01111111)
